PORTFOLIO

Physics Puzzle Game

Unreal Engine 4/5

Solve puzzles using by combining multiple abilities. These abilities include telekinesis, Magnetism, Metal Wall Attach, Time Stop, Time Reverse, Scale Change and Portals. Each puzzle is different from the other, and there are more than one way to solve a puzzle. This game consists of more than 200 unique puzzles with an average gameplay duration of 6-8 hrs.
