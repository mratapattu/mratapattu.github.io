ROLE: Unreal Engine Developer

I am the Lead Developer of this PC VR experience which recreates a historically accurate version of Sigiriya during 500CE, which is a UNESCO world heritage site in Sri Lanka. We plan to expand to other locations in Sri Lanka and eventually recreate foreign historical sites. This was developed using UE5. The project has been deployed in the lobby area of Cinnamon Grand Colombo and Habarana Village by Cinnamon.

Responsibilities:

  • Fixing visual and technical issues in Rendering for VR. This included resolving issues such as flickering, shadow artifacts, and other visual glitches.
  • Creating and rendering cinematic sequences. Many gameplay events are triggered throughout these sequences, such as lighting changes, birds flying near the camera, pots falling into water, and character actions.
  • Visual Optimization.
  • General programming tasks such as switching between past and present, player interactions and more.
  • Work related to materials and particle effects.
  • Implemented Perforce version control and set up workstation networking for file sharing and access control.
  • Integrating animations, sounds, and UI.
  • Creating plugins for easier use for artists.
  • Designing and implementing an efficient development pipeline.
