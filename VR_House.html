PORTFOLIO

VR House Building Simulation

Unreal Engine 4 | 2023

  • Project Walkthrough Video

ROLE: Sole Developer

This is a training simulation to build a house in virtual reality. The player will attach different parts together using nails and bolts to form larger parts. Players can highlight the next part to attach, and repetitive actions are automatically completed. The product was developed and successfully delivered to the client.
