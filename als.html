Niro 2 Game Development

Unreal 5

Worked on developing a sequel to the Niro video game, focusing on integrating the Advanced Locomotion System for characters and enhancing the game with a more complex AI system. I contributed to creating an advanced character animation system tailored to support a more complex AI framework. This system is designed to handle interactions among players, allies, enemies, and civilians in a dynamic and immersive way. The game features a group of up to four characters, which can be controlled by players or AI, engaging in combat with enemy characters. Civilians populate the game world, adding depth and realism. We developed an AI system where each character intelligently reacts to others, dynamically adapting their behavior to achieve individual goals.
